💾 Backup & Restore

Export weekly, save to Google Drive. Import to restore if PC breaks.

☁️ Google Drive — Real Auto-Upload

When connected, every saved PDF automatically uploads in the background to your Google Drive under graywolflogistics2026 documents/, organized by the document's own date: settlements go to <Month>/Payroll/Week-1..5/, purchases to <Month>/Equipment-Deductions/<Store>/ (Amazon, Home Depot, etc.), and Fuel / Maintenance / Tolls / Bank Statements each get their own monthly folder. No download dialogs, no manual steps.

📁 Auto-Organize Imported PDFs (local fallback)

If Google Drive isn't connected above, PDFs auto-download instead with an organized name — e.g. 2026-06/Payroll/2026-06-28_settlement_prime-inc.pdf. Point your browser's download destination at your Google Drive sync folder to get the same result without connecting.

ℹ️ One-time setup (Chrome/Edge): Settings → Downloads → turn on "Ask where to save each file before downloading". First time you save, pick your Google Drive folder — the browser remembers it after that, so future saves need just one click.
